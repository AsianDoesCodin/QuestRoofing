FAQ

Quick answers for roof repair, replacement, inspection, and Arizona roof life questions.

How much does a new roof cost in the Phoenix area?

Roof replacement cost depends on size, material, access, and complexity. We provide a written estimate so you can see the real project number before work starts.

How long does a roof last in Arizona's desert climate?

Arizona roof life depends on material, sun exposure, installation quality, maintenance, and coating or underlayment condition.

Do you offer free inspections and written estimates?

Yes. We can start inspection requests with photos and a written estimate so the scope is clear.

Call us quickly for active leaks. During monsoon conditions, we prioritize urgent roof leaks when scheduling allows.

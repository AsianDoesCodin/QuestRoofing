FAQ

Questions homeowners ask before they book an inspection.

Quick answers for roof repair, replacement, inspection, and Arizona roof life questions.

How much does a new roof cost in the East Valley?

Roof replacement cost depends on size, material, access, and complexity. A written estimate gives the real project number before work starts.

How long does a roof last in Arizona's desert climate?

Arizona roof life depends on material, sun exposure, installation quality, maintenance, and coating or underlayment condition.

Do you offer free inspections and written estimates?

Yes. Inspection requests can start with photos and a written estimate so the scope is clear.

How fast can you respond to an active leak?

Active leaks should be called in quickly. During monsoon conditions, urgent roof leaks are prioritized when scheduling allows.
